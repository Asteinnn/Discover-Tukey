PORT BOSPHOROUS

Port Bosphorus Hotel is a stylish boutique hotel located in the vibrant Ortaköy neighborhood of Istanbul, offering breathtaking views of the Bosphorus Strait. The hotel features modern, elegantly designed rooms and suites, many equipped with private balconies to soak in the stunning scenery. Guests can indulge in a delightful dining experience at the on-site restaurant, which serves a mix of traditional Turkish and international cuisine, complemented by a cozy bar for relaxation. Amenities include a well-equipped fitness center and a serene spa, providing guests with opportunities to unwind after exploring the city. The hotel's prime location makes it easy to access iconic attractions such as Ortaköy Mosque and Dolmabahçe Palace, as well as bustling shopping areas and vibrant nightlife. With its blend of contemporary comfort and cultural charm, Port Bosphorus Hotel is an excellent choice for travelers seeking a memorable stay in Istanbul while enjoying modern luxury and stunning views. HOTEL SEASHELL RESORT AND SPA

otel Seashell Resort and Spa is a luxurious beachfront destination located in the beautiful coastal town of Bodrum, Turkey. This resort offers a blend of modern elegance and traditional charm, featuring spacious rooms and suites that overlook the sparkling Aegean Sea. Each accommodation is designed for comfort, equipped with contemporary amenities and private balconies. Guests can indulge in a variety of dining options, ranging from local Turkish cuisine to international favorites, all served in stylish settings. The resort boasts a stunning spa, offering a range of treatments and wellness programs, along with multiple swimming pools and a private beach for relaxation. For those seeking adventure, Hotel Seashell provides various water sports and activities. With its idyllic location, top-notch facilities, and attentive service, this resort is perfect for couples, families, or anyone looking to unwind and enjoy the stunning beauty of Bodrum’s coastline. URLA SAHANE HOTEL

Urla Sahane Hotel is a charming boutique hotel located in the picturesque coastal town of Urla, Turkey. Nestled amidst lush gardens and close to the Aegean Sea, this hotel offers a serene escape with a blend of modern comfort and traditional design. Each of the elegantly appointed rooms features contemporary amenities, ensuring a relaxing stay for guests. The hotel boasts a delightful on-site restaurant that serves delicious local cuisine, highlighting the flavors of the region. Guests can unwind by the outdoor pool or explore the nearby beaches and charming local markets. The hotel's welcoming atmosphere and attentive staff make it a perfect choice for travelers seeking both tranquility and an authentic Turkish experience. With its prime location, Urla Sahane Hotel is also conveniently situated for exploring nearby attractions, including vineyards and historical sites, making it an ideal base for discovering the beauty of the Aegean region.
