Nature & Envirnoment



Discover the breathtaking beauty and rich biodiversity of Turkey, where nature and history intertwine seamlessly.







SAKLI CAPI CANYON

Saklı Cennet Canyon , often referred to as "Hidden Paradise," is a stunning natural wonder located in the Çamlıhemşin district of Turkey's Rize province. This picturesque canyon is renowned for its breathtaking landscapes, lush greenery, and crystal-clear waters. Surrounded by towering cliffs and dense forests, it offers visitors a serene escape into nature. The canyon is perfect for hiking, with well-marked trails that wind through the vibrant flora and fauna of the region. Adventurers can also enjoy activities like canyoning and swimming in the refreshing pools formed by the flowing waters. Saklı Cennet is not just a haven for nature lovers; it’s also rich in cultural heritage, with nearby traditional villages showcasing the region's unique architecture and local cuisine. Whether you’re seeking adventure or tranquility, Saklı Cennet Canyon provides a captivating experience, making it a must-visit destination for anyone exploring the enchanting landscapes of northeastern Turkey.


CAPPADOCIA

Cappadocia, located in central Turkey, is a captivating region famous for its unique geological formations, including the iconic fairy chimneys—tall, thin spires of rock shaped by volcanic activity. A highlight of visiting Cappadocia is hot air ballooning at sunrise, providing breathtaking views of the surreal landscape. The *Göreme Open-Air Museum*, a UNESCO World Heritage site, features ancient rock-cut churches adorned with stunning frescoes from the Byzantine era. Visitors can explore the fascinating underground cities of Derinkuyu and Kaymaklı, where early Christians once sought refuge. The *Ihlara Valley* offers beautiful hiking trails alongside a river, dotted with more rock-cut churches. Charming villages like Ürgüp and Avanos showcase local craftsmanship, particularly in pottery. With its blend of natural beauty, rich history, and cultural experiences, Cappadocia is a must-visit destination for anyone seeking adventure and enchantment in Turkey.



LAKE SALDA

*Lake Salda* is a stunning freshwater lake located in southwestern Turkey, near the town of Burdur. Often referred to as "Turkey's Maldives," it is famous for its striking turquoise waters and white sandy beaches. The lake's unique high magnesium content creates a vibrant color, complemented by beautiful white mineral formations along the shoreline. Surrounded by picturesque landscapes, including rolling hills and forests, Lake Salda is perfect for nature lovers. Visitors can enjoy swimming, sunbathing, and picnicking, with shallow waters ideal for families. The area is also a haven for birdwatchers and photographers, thanks to its diverse flora and fauna. Efforts are being made to preserve the lake's ecological integrity, protecting it from pollution and overdevelopment. With its breathtaking scenery and serene atmosphere, Lake Salda offers a tranquil escape, making it a must-visit destination for those exploring the natural beauty of Turkey.
